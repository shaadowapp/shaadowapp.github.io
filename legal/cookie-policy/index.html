Cookie Policy

March 30, 2026

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us understand how you interact with our services and provide a more personalized experience.

2. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function. They allow us to remember your session and preferences.

  • Authentication: Used to maintain your logged-in state on our websites.
  • Preferences: Used to remember your dark/light mode choices or language settings.

3. Analytics and Performance

We may use cookies to understand how our websites are being used. This information is anonymous and helps us improve our digital experience.

4. Your Choice

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can choose to block or delete cookies at any time. Please note that blocking essential cookies may affect the functionality of our websites.
