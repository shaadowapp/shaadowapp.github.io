Child Safety Standards

March 30, 2026

1. Our Commitment

At Shaadow Platforms, the safety and well-being of minors are our highest priorities. We are strictly committed to providing a safe digital environment for all users, especially children.

2. Prohibited Content

The following are strictly prohibited on all Shaadow Platform products:

  • Child Exploitation: Any content related to child abuse, exploitation, or imagery.
  • Harassment: Bullying or targeting of minors.
  • Grooming: Content or behavior intended to manipulate or solicit minors.

3. Reporting and Enforcement

We employ robust reporting mechanisms on Boofer to identify and remove harmful content.

  • Fast Response: Reports related to child safety are prioritized and addressed within 24 hours.
  • Zero Tolerance: Violations of these standards result in immediate and permanent bans without warning.
  • Legal Cooperation: We cooperate with law enforcement agencies and organizations (e.g., NCMEC) to report child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

4. Parental Guidance

We encourage parents to monitor their children's digital activity. While Boofer is encrypted, the Discover section is public. We recommend that minors use these services only under parental supervision.

Have a concern? Report it to us immediately.
